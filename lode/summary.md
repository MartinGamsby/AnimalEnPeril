Animal en Péril is a French-language 2D canvas platformer where the player controls an animal through gravity-flipping, dimension-shifting levels collecting orbs to reach portals. Built with vanilla JS (no framework), loaded via `<script>` tags in index.html. Features 13 main levels + 4 challenge levels, 10 unlockable animals, a shop/coin system, star ratings, lasers, and touch/keyboard controls.
